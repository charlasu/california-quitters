Why Quit California?




In reaction to a Los Angles Times article discussing California emigration, this project explores what might be influencing it and the influence it might be having in turn. Because the article also mentions the amenities migrating Californians supposedly expect, migration data from California is surveyed alongside a favorite amenity's growth in several Western states: Washington, Oregon, Colorado, New Mexico, Utah, Idaho, Nevada and Arizona.
